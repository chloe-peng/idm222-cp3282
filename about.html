On a more personal level

If you met me about 5 years ago, I would've never imagined myself "here." After graduating from a STEM focused high school, creative fields felt like a mile away. But between my love of playing the piano and dancing on stage, I realized they weren't as far as I thought.

My strongest tool: Curiousity

The root of design lies in diversifying our own human experiences. Back in NYC, I saw the unique experiences of communities I cross paths with go through that get glossed over. Ever since moving to Philly, my drive to change the ways human interact has only grown with the rise of new technologies.

Moving Forward

I've always been passionate about reconnecting with my heritage. I spend most of my free time working alongside cultural organizations or learning Cantonese to expand my knowledge and scope of life. By doing so, I'm dedicated to finding the universal language that everyone can speak to.
